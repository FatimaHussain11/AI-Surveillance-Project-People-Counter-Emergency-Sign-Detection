# **AI Surveillance Project** :rotating_light::busts_in_silhouette:

[![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3.10-blue?logo=python&logoColor=white)](https://www.python.org/)

**AI Surveillance Project** is an intelligent monitoring system designed to enhance **safety** and **situational awareness** in public and private spaces. It combines **people counting** and **emergency sign detection** to provide real-time alerts and monitoring, making it ideal for surveillance, crowd management, and emergency response.  

---

## **Features** :sparkles:
- **People Counting:** Detects and counts individuals in frames or video streams to monitor crowd density and occupancy.  
- **Emergency Sign Detection:** Recognizes critical signs like **"Help," "Danger,"** or **"Peace"**, sending instant alerts.  
- **Surveillance Camera Integration:** Works with both **live cameras** and pre-recorded videos.  
- **AI-Powered Detection:** Utilizes **deep learning models** for fast and accurate performance in various environments.  
- **Real-Time Monitoring:** Alerts authorities or personnel immediately upon detecting emergencies or abnormal crowd density.
